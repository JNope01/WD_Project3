Survival Tips&Trainings

12 tips for surviving a zombie apocalypse:

  1. Separate friends from foes: You can’t survive an apocalypse alone. Build a small, close-knit team of allies.
  2. Set up a base: Find a safe place before dark where you can plan and sleep without fear of zombie interruption.
  3. Make hunting for supplies a priority: Prioritize finding food, first aid kits, lighters, sleeping bags, and more.
  4. Don’t drink the water: Bottled water is crucial. An infectious virus may be in the water. Hand sanitizer is also essential.
  5. Pull your weight: Make yourself indispensable to your survival group to avoid being abandoned.
  6. Eliminate the weakest link: If a team member is a liability, it's acceptable to abandon them to their fate.
  7. Find the perfect zombie-fighting ensemble: Protective clothing is essential to avoid blood splatter. Goggles, gloves, and a helmet are recommended.
  8. Just because you have shelter doesn’t mean you’re safe: Zombies can break through barriers, especially in large numbers.
  9. Never turn your back on a zombie: Zombies can move quickly, especially at night.
  10. Find ways to fight boredom: Create your own entertainment, and personal dramas can be a source of distraction.
  11. Be ready for anything: Prepare physically and psychologically for the unknown nature of a zombie apocalypse.
  12. Beware of humans: In an apocalypse, friends can become foes quickly. Trust no one blindly.
